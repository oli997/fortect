Requirements Before Logging In
==============================

Before attempting to log in, make sure you have the following:

Having these ready will help ensure a smooth login process.

How to Log In to Your Fortect Account
=====================================

Follow these steps to access your account.

Step 1: Open Your Preferred Browser
-----------------------------------

Launch a modern web browser such as Chrome, Firefox, Edge, or Safari.
Using the latest browser version helps improve compatibility and security.

Step 2: Visit the Official Fortect Website
------------------------------------------

Navigate to the official Fortect website and locate the login or sign-in
section.

Always verify that you are visiting the official website to avoid phishing
attempts.

Step 3: Click the Login Button
------------------------------

Locate the **Log In**, **Sign In**, or **My Account** option on the homepage.

Clicking this option will take you to the account authentication page.

Step 4: Enter Your Email Address
--------------------------------

Type the email address used when creating your Fortect account.

Ensure there are no typing mistakes or unnecessary spaces.

Step 5: Enter Your Password
---------------------------

Carefully enter your password.

Remember that passwords are case-sensitive, so verify that Caps Lock is not
enabled.

Step 6: Complete Any Verification
---------------------------------

Depending on your account settings, you may be required to complete additional
security verification before gaining access.

Follow the on-screen instructions if prompted.

Step 7: Access Your Dashboard
-----------------------------

After successful authentication, your account dashboard will open.

From here, you can manage your subscription, download software, update your
information, and review account settings.

How to Reset a Forgotten Password
=================================

If you cannot remember your password, recovering your account is usually
straightforward.

Step 1
------

Go to the login page.

Step 2
------

Select the **Forgot Password** option.

Step 3
------

Enter your registered email address.

Step 4
------

Submit the password recovery request.

Step 5
------

Check your email inbox for password reset instructions.

Step 6
------

Follow the provided link and create a strong new password.

Step 7
------

Return to the login page and sign in using your updated credentials.

Tips for Creating a Strong Password
===================================

A secure password reduces the risk of unauthorized account access.

Consider these recommendations:

- Use at least twelve characters.
- Include uppercase letters.
- Include lowercase letters.
- Add numbers.
- Include special characters.
- Avoid personal information.
- Do not reuse passwords from other websites.
- Update passwords periodically.

Common Fortect Login Problems
=============================

Although the login process is generally simple, users may occasionally
experience difficulties.

Incorrect Password
------------------

Double-check your password for spelling errors.

Email Address Not Recognized
----------------------------

Ensure you are using the same email address that was used during registration.

Browser Cache Issues
--------------------

Clear your browser's cache and cookies before trying again.

Internet Connectivity Problems
------------------------------

Verify that your internet connection is stable.

Temporary Website Maintenance
-----------------------------

Occasionally, maintenance may temporarily affect login availability.

Wait a short period before attempting to sign in again.

Account Locked
--------------

Multiple unsuccessful login attempts may temporarily restrict account access.

Wait for the lockout period to expire or contact customer support if necessary.

Browser Compatibility
---------------------

If login problems continue, try another supported browser or update your
existing browser.
